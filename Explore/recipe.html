Recipe Details

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix in the cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some pasta water, and mix with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
